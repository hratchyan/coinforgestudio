Everything a coin needs

Purpose-built tools instead of wrestling a general-purpose vector editor.

Ring designer

One-click curated frames — star circles, rope, beaded, reeded mint edge, chains, laurel wreaths, knockout-star military bands. Every part stays editable.

Coin-true arc text

Top and bottom text that behaves like real coinage — upright both sides, baseline-true radii, justify-across-arc, bare-metal knockouts on marked bands.

Smart photo import

Paste a screenshot or drop a photo. AI background removal runs locally — nothing is uploaded — then your subject lands centered and sized.

Templates & symbols

16 complete coin layouts and 90+ curated vector symbols — badges, wreaths, maltese crosses, anchors, compasses — plus hundreds of ornament glyphs.

Multi-pass export

Group elements into engraving passes, export one aligned file per pass or a color-mapped SVG your laser software auto-splits. PNG at exact DPI.

Cut outline generator

One click traces a smooth vector silhouette around your whole design — even when the art has no continuous edge — ready for the cutting layer.

Three steps to a coin

Design

Start from a template or a blank. Add rings, text, symbols — or paste a photo and let Smart Import do the cutout.

Export

PNG at 1016 DPI (0.025 mm/px), dithered or grayscale, sized exactly to your blank. SVG for vector workflows.

Engrave

Import into LightBurn, WeCreat MakeIt, or any laser software — don't rescale — and burn. The built-in guide covers settings.
